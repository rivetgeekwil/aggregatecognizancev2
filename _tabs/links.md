## Cortex
- [Cortex Lite](https://xineink.itch.io/lite). Free community-created build of the Cortex Prime RPG.
- [Cortex Prime](https://www.cortexrpg.com/). Multi-genre, modular, session-centered roleplaying game from Fandom Tabletop
- [Keystone](https://joshroby.com/keystone/). Cortex Prime-powered fantasy RPG from Miriam Robern, designed to gradually introduce Cortex concepts.
- [Tales of Xadia](https://www.talesofxadia.com/). The official Dragon Prince RPG powered by Cortex.
- [Tim Bannock](https://timbannock.com/category/resources/). Cortex Prime and other rpg goodness from Tim Bannock. 
- [Xine](https://xine.ink/). Free community-created xine for the Cortex Prime RPG.
